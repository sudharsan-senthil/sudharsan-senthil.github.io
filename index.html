Welcome to my site. I'm Sudharsan Senthil, a dual degree student at IIT Madras, pursuing M.Tech in Electrical Engineering. I'm interested in understanding the convergence of stochastic optimization algorithms especially the ones used in ML, and I'm also keen on solving fundamental information theory problems arising in communication and compression. I was very lucky to have worked under Prof. Nambi Seshadri and Prof. David Koilpillai on data-driven learning of channel coding for interfering users. And, as a rising senior, I interned at Washington University in St. Louis, working with Prof. Yiannis Kantaros on developing a temporal logic planning toolbox for robots swarm. Currently, I'm adviced by Prof. Anant Raj broadly on utilizing Lyapunov potential to track the SGLD optimization. Apart from academics, I'm an avid reader, and a board games enthusiast.
